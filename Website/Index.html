Project Summary

Embark on an exciting engineering challenge with our Hyperion Autonomous Rover. Pushing the boundaries of robotics, this project challenges students to design, build, and test an autonomous rover capable of navigating rough terrain, avoiding obstacles, and reaching precise GPS waypoints. With real-world constraints like RF communication and weather resistance, the rover must perform mission-critical tasks with accuracy and efficiency.
