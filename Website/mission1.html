Mission 1 Report

Rolling Chassis Requirements

The rolling chassis must successfully complete two consecutive runs to meet the requirements.

Task 1: Square Pattern Navigation

  • The rover must drive in an approximately 10-foot square pattern.
  • Upon completion, it should stop at its original location and place a marker.
  • The marker should be within 3 feet of the rover’s original starting position.
  • The mission should be completed within 60 seconds.

Task 2: Straight-Line Distance Navigation

  • The rover must drive straight for a specified distance between 15 and 30 feet.
  • It must place a physical marker at the designated stopping point.
  • The exact intended distance will be transmitted from an RF transmitter beacon at the starting location.
  • The marker placement must be within ±10% of the specified drive distance.
  • The task must be completed in under 30 seconds.

RF Distance Transmission

  • The distance to drive will be transmitted as a 12-character message.
  • The format consists of the distance as two digits, preceded by an ‘X’, with the remaining characters padded with ‘Z’.
  • For example, a message instructing the rover to travel 15 feet would be: X15ZZZZZZZZZ.
  • The intended distance will be continuously transmitted from the RF beacon at 5-second intervals.
